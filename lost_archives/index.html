The Great War Experience: Your Choices, Your Survival, Your Legacy

Step into the trenches of World War I, where every decision can mean the difference between life and death. Whether you fight on the frontlines or manage supplies behind the scenes, your role is crucial in this immersive war-torn world. Brace yourself for the horrors and triumphs of one of history's most devastating conflicts.

Live or Die by Your Actions

In the chaos of war, every moment counts. There are no arbitrary rules here—only the real consequences of your actions. With perma-death in play, every decision on the battlefield has weight. If your character falls in combat, they are gone, and you must wait 24 hours before rejoining the fight with a new identity. It's war—make your choices wisely.

Join the Army: Discipline or Desperation?

Will you serve honorably in the ranks of your nation’s army, or take desperate measures to survive in the chaos? From infantry soldiers to medics and supply officers, every role matters. Join your comrades, defend your trench, and carry out the orders that could lead to victory—or defeat.

Supply & Demand: The Lifeblood of War

War isn't won solely on the battlefield—it’s won through logistics. Manage the critical supplies needed to keep your army equipped and fed. Trade, negotiate, or even sabotage the enemy’s resources. Control the flow of materials, and you might just control the outcome of the war.

Unique Features:

  • Dynamic Battlefield: Frontlines shift based on player decisions and battles.
  • Permanent Character Loss: High-stakes roleplay with real consequences.
  • Realistic Military Hierarchy: Join the ranks, rise through the chain of command, or face military justice.
  • Supply Management: Control resources for your faction and influence the war effort.
  • Immersive Environment: Custom maps reflecting the trenches, war-torn villages, and fields of Europe.

No pay-to-win, no unfair advantages—just you, your comrades, and the brutal reality of war. Whether you're on the frontlines or behind the scenes, every role is vital in this immersive World War I experience.

Whose side are you on? For which country do you fight? The war is calling—choose your allegiance and make history on the battlefield. United by honor, divided by war.
