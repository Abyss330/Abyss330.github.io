Background to RP

The year is 1914, and Europe stands on the brink of the most devastating conflict in human history. Tensions have been rising between the great powers—Germany, Austria-Hungary, Russia, France, and Britain—fueled by decades of imperialism, alliances, and militarization. The assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand of Austria by a Serbian nationalist is the spark that ignites the fire, plunging the world into a war unlike any ever seen before.

As the war unfolds, millions of soldiers from all corners of the world are called to arms. Trenches are dug from the English Channel to the Swiss Alps, creating a vast and brutal frontline where soldiers endure unimaginable horrors: artillery bombardments, gas attacks, and deadly charges across no man’s land. The war isn’t just fought on the battlefield—nations mobilize their economies, industries, and civilians in a desperate bid to win. The world is forever changed by this "war to end all wars."
